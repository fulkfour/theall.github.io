Welcome to StringEx home page!

StringEx is a utility for merging horizontal text.It can easily pick up distinct portion from multi-line text, and merge up to 4 columns a time with a small template.With StringEx's help,you can improve the efficiency of inputing large text which including much redundant content, and let you make less mistakes caused by hand inputing.


  • Merge up to 4 columns of horizontal text with a small template.
  • Easily split block text into distinct columns of text.
  • Match multi-groups in text with regular expression.
  • Quick assemble up to 4 columns.
  • Quick general number sequence,alphabetic sequence,date sequence,time sequence and random data.
